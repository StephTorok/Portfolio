Short Film

The Metal Sculptures of Borrego Springs

This short documentary was a co-production with Laura Ganz-Holtan. It focuses on the large steel welded sculptures located throughout the desert community of Borrego Springs, California, which were created by the artist Ricardo Breceda.

It was intended to be an entertaining documentary to illustrate some of the real and imagined animals as well as some of the people that have inhabited the Anza Borrego Desert in ancient and present times.

Tasks for this project included:

Video Editing
Audio Editing
Narration
Animations
Video Footage
Photography

Animations

Inspired by the clay animation comedy Creature Comforts the metal sculptures were brought to life by animations and narrate the story themselves.
