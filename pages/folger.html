Der Folger

This video was pro bono work for the Austrian band Stringulatur. They wanted a video for Austrian Music Television to promote their newly released album Besser So.

Concept & Design

The band had no budget for a video available and couldnâ€™t supply any footage apart from their concert recording. The focus was therefore put on their album art work as the main inspiration for the video. Even though some of the lyrics/keywords were purposely supported by matching visuals, the main intend was to create an overall atmosphere that transports the feel of the song and to visually pick up on the album cover to create unity and increase brand recognition.
