Logo Animation

Bold Bear Graphics

The animated logo consists of a bear within circles and rounded rectangles that depict a camera lens with an attached viewfinder.

The concept was to animate it in a fun and playful way. The background is a beach scene with a setting sun and a camera which takes pictures of the bear who plays around with the individual shapes that will eventually come together as the whole logo.

The Logo

The logo itself was designed for a small business called Bold Bear Graphics that sells postcards, art prints, unique souvenirs, upcycled accent furniture, and clothing. Since these are very different items that are difficult to visualize as an entity, it was designed as a more universal logo that expresses the company name rather than its products. The design incorparates camera features since the camera is the main tool used for creating most of the products.

It was designed to be versatile, fun, bold and solid so it can be used as a clothing label of about 1x1 inches as well as a stamp image on the back of postcards. It can be used in different color schemes to attract different audiences.

The Music

The music was created in a digital music creation studio using a loop and several soundeffects like a camera shutter, seagulls and ocean waves.
