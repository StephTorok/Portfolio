Movie Trailer

Raw

This one-minute long movie trailer was created for a ficticious TV Series or drama that is set in the club scene of Berlin, Germany - in particular the club RAW and other adjacent clubs that have recently seen an increase in crime and drug dealings.

It is inspired by the intros of the HBO Series True Detective. The challenge was to create a dark, mystic atmosphere with imagery using double exposure effects and dark yet upbeat music.

The Music

The selected song Between the West and the setting Sun by the band Ghoultown was chosen in the beginning of the creative process and greatly inspired the mood, the selected video footage and the pace of the final piece.
